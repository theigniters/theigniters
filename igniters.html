About The Igniters

This MOVEMENT is organized to glorify God by conducting religious services, and teaching and training people in the Truth of God’s Word, the Bible, calling people to repentance from sin and to faith in Christ, making disciples of our Lord Jesus Christ, and edifying the Body of Christ. The Igniters is a youth movement dedicated to igniting revival—helping people discover purpose, equipping them to make an impact and witnessing for Jesus. We are dedicated to raising people who will influence all systems of the world for the Lord Jesus.

Our mission To be active in soul winning and edification of the saints through diverse means and equipping the young for end-time exploits.

our vision is to see a generation of young people burning for Jesus, taking the Gospel in excellence to all nations—influencing all spheres of influence with Godly values.

What We Do

Spiritual Growth

Weekly Bible studies, prayer meetings, and spiritual retreats designed to deepen faith and understanding.

Community & Fellowship

Fun social events, team-building activities, and mentorship programs to build lasting friendships.

Leadership Development

Workshops, training sessions, and opportunities to lead and serve within the Movement and community.

Outreach & Service

Engaging in local community service projects, missions, and acts of kindness to make a tangible difference.

Education & Mentorship

Guidance on academic pursuits, career paths, and life skills from experienced mentors.

Arts & Creativity

Opportunities to express faith and talent through music, drama, visual arts, and more.
